Revolutionising the Gym

Welcome to Ascend Gym, Newcastle upon Tyne’s fitness luxury spot. It is an understatement to say that Ascend redefines gym experience because it is a sanctuary for health enthusiasts. The state-of-the-art facility will take your fitness journey to the next level and provide you with an unrivaled atmosphere for your wellness goals.

Ascend Gym is much more than a workout place; it is a lifestyle destination that combines state-of-the-art equipment with friendly environment. We are committed to creating an inclusive community at our gym because we consider ourselves as the best fitness centre in Newcastle. Whether you are an active enthusiast or just starting your journey towards well-being, Ascend will embrace you.

Everyone is welcome

Step into a premium fitness experience in the heart of Newcastle upon Tyne, where we redefine the gym scene by combining luxury with results. Our cutting-edge facility is designed for individuals of all backgrounds and fitness levels, creating a diverse and dynamic community.

Inclusivity is more than a buzzword here – it's a fundamental part of our ethos. We're proud to welcome everyone, whether you're a seasoned fitness enthusiast, a newcomer to the scene, or someone returning after a break. Our team of trainers and staff is committed to ensuring that you feel at ease and motivated throughout your fitness journey.

Here to help

At our fitness sanctuary, we understand that everyone's fitness journey is unique, and that's why we're here to offer personalized support every step of the way. Our team of dedicated onsite personal trainers and coaches is committed to helping you achieve your fitness goals, tailoring plans to your individual needs.

Imagine having a fitness partner who understands your aspirations, challenges, and preferences. Our experienced personal trainers are here to be just that. Whether you're aiming for weight loss, muscle gain, or overall well-being, they'll work closely with you to create a customized workout and nutrition plan that aligns with your objectives.
